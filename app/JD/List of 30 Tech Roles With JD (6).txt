I. Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
1. AI/ML Engineer: Designs and deploys the models that power intelligent systems.
2. Generative AI Engineer: Specializes in Large Language Models (LLMs) and RAG architecture.
3. NLP (Natural Language Processing) Specialist: Focuses on human-computer language interaction.
4. Computer Vision Engineer: Develops systems that interpret visual data (used in medical imaging/self-driving cars).
5. AI Ethics & Compliance Officer: Ensures AI models are unbiased and follow global regulations.
6. AI Product Manager: Translates AI capabilities into marketable business features.
7. Machine Learning Scientist: Conducts advanced research to create new ML algorithms.
8. Robotics Engineer: Integrates AI with physical hardware for automation and manufacturing.
II. Data Science & Big Data
9. Data Scientist: Uses advanced statistics and ML to find patterns in complex datasets.
10. Data Engineer: Builds the heavy-duty pipelines that move and clean raw data.
11. Big Data Architect: Designs large-scale distributed systems to store massive amounts of information.
12. Business Intelligence (BI) Analyst: Visualizes data to help executives make strategic decisions.
13. Data Governance Specialist: Manages data privacy, quality, and organizational standards.
III. Software Engineering & Development
14. Full-Stack Developer: Proficient in both frontend (user interface) and backend (logic/databases).
15. Backend Engineer: Focuses on server-side architecture, APIs, and performance.
16. Frontend Engineer: Specializes in creating responsive, high-performance user interfaces.
17. Mobile App Developer: Builds native or cross-platform apps for iOS and Android.
18. Blockchain Developer: Develops decentralized applications (DApps) and smart contracts.
19. Embedded Systems Engineer: Writes low-level code for hardware devices and IoT sensors.
20. Game Developer: Uses engines like Unity or Unreal to build interactive experiences.
IV. Cloud, DevOps & Infrastructure
21. Cloud Solutions Architect: Designs the overall cloud strategy and infrastructure for an organization.
22. DevOps Engineer: Automates the software delivery pipeline (CI/CD) and improves deployment speed.
23. Site Reliability Engineer (SRE): Uses software engineering to solve infrastructure and operations problems.
24. Platform Engineer: Builds the internal platforms that other developers use to be productive.
25. FinOps Specialist: A hybrid role managing the financial optimization of cloud spending.
V. Cybersecurity & Trust
26. Cybersecurity Analyst: Monitors and defends networks against real-time threats.
27. Ethical Hacker (Penetration Tester): Proactively attacks systems to find and fix vulnerabilities.
28. Application Security Engineer: Ensures that security is built into the software code itself.
VI. Experience & Strategy
29. UI/UX Designer: Researches and designs the flow and look of digital products.
30. IT Project Manager: Oversees technical teams to ensure projects are delivered on time and within budget.
